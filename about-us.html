WHAT IS ARDO?

Amaravati Rare Diseases Organisation with registered number 32/2019 is a Non-governmental Organisation registered as Section 25 company working for people suffering from a rare disease known as muscular dystrophy in India set up in 2019.

Our organisation aims at providing care and nurtures the patients by distributing then nutritious food for survival, wheelchairs for locomotion, organizing awareness camps, diagnosis tests and physio-therapy treatment. ARDO has also been very proactive to do and help the society in the way possible not just limiting to its core activities as it believes helping each other and everyone would entirely make the world a better place to live in.In the wake of Covid-19, ARDO has been very active in organizing food distribution drive and providing food to the homeless destitute by associating with City Police Department. It has also distributed sanitizers and other essentials to the police department.

The main focus of ARDO is on the people suffering with a rare disease called muscular dystrophy. Muscular Dystrophy is a genetic disease that causes progressive weakness and loss of muscle mass. There is no medicinal cure for this. In addition there may arise other complications affecting kidneys, heart and lungs causing death of the person.
